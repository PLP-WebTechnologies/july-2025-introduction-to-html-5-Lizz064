What Is Clean Energy?

Clean energy refers to energy derived from renewable, zero-emissions sources that do not pollute the atmosphere when used. It's essential for reducing climate change and building a sustainable future.

Types of Clean Energy

Solar Power

Solar panels convert sunlight into electricity, offering a clean and abundant source of energy for homes and businesses.

Wind Energy

Wind turbines capture wind flow to generate power, making it one of the fastest-growing renewable technologies globally.

Hydropower

Hydropower uses flowing water to produce energy. It's reliable and efficient, especially in regions with rivers and dams.

Why It Matters

Clean energy plays a crucial role in reducing greenhouse gas emissions, improving public health, and creating a resilient energy future for the planet.
